I am Mason Qualls I made this site to help those who are going through the loss of what is important to you or those around you.

The loss of one that is close to you is something that is really hard to actually face, due to the fact that it holds a lot of emotional pain and for some physical.

The Problem

Loss can affect almost everyone. It also affects everyone differently some may need to cry and not mention it for a while, some may need to let out their sadness through anger and fury, and others may just deny it entirely. Grief is something everyone faces and goes through at least once in their life. It is something that has a heavy and large effect on people when it does happen to people. Grief comes in 5 stages Anger, Bargaining, Denial, Depression, and Acceptance. These five stages could happen in any order and effect everyone differently.

Mental

The mental aspect of this is through some facing depression while going through grief and facing each step with more tears.

Physical

Due to your emotions no longer in check you may feel tired and less willing to do complete daily routines or basic tasks.

Indirect

You can be effected directly or through another because of their emotions being so unstable they may be suseptable to mood swings.

Importance to People

Change

Loss and Death can change people befor ethey even know it and the changes it makes are subtle but are noticable with time.

Experience

The experience you have when facing the loss of something close is hard to deal with. For some people they cry it away for others the face grief for a long time.

Solutions

Optional solutions that you can try.

Talk About It

Talking about the reason for your grief can help you get over it faster because you can get all of the stages out of your systemuntil you finally accept they are gone.

Therapy

Talking to a therapist will allow someone with a degree for human mental health would help a lot more because of their skills and diagnosis' would be much more accurate than an opinion.

Indirect Affect

If you are being indirectly affected then help your loved one by making sure they're comfortable, are mentally sound, and are fine with your presense.
